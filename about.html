We turned traditional ideas about debt upside down

Have you ever noticed how creditors such as banks and retailers shun you when you owe them money? Well, ShopperFund creator and industry pioneer David G. Rosenberg thinks that's backwards. After all, you're still a customer and you need help! ShopperFund was founded by a team of financial service professionals who got together to create ways to help consumers (instead of shunning them) overcome credit card debt and learn from financial challenges.

Our mission

It's our mission to create an innovative ecommerce solution to help you reduce your debt painlessly and help make debt reduction and financial stability a reality. We set out to find a way that retailers could help consumers pay down their debt.

How we did it

When you buy gift cards to your favorite retailers through a free ShopperFund account and use them to buy everyday items such as food, clothing and toiletries, you earn cash-back rewards credits that can only be applied directly toward paying a debt or any bill. This way, maybe you can pay a little more toward your debt, but without hurting your budget. And, we created a personal finance blog where finances are made simpler and the ways out of debt and towards smart spending are made clearer. You can also hear stories of different ways to use your free ShopperFund account to maximize your debt payments. We encourage you to ask us financial questions (and we can answer them on the blog!) and to reach out to us via Twitter @ShopperFund or via email at help@shopperfund.com
